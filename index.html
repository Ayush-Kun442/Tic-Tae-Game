How to play

  • Select your mode: battle a friend or the built-in computer.
  • Online mode lets you create a room ID to share with a friend. The creator plays X, the guest plays O.
  • Click any square to place your mark. X always goes first.
  • The first player with three marks in a row wins the round.
  • Use Reset Game anytime to start over and keep the scores.
